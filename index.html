Who We Are

Family owned and run since it's inception, Cerious Technologies is the brainchild of Robert Grost, a structural engineer and synthetic materials pioneer. After devising the world's first set of speakers designed with fluid filled stainless steel tubes and a cast ballistic ceramic layer, with a final skin of carbon fiber, the Cerious One was born. Cerious Technologies started the day these speakers were hooked up.

Cerious is a leader in the audio equipment community, focused on never-before-heard quality at an affordable price. Our cables are the best you'll find, and each one is handmade by our president and founder himself.

Graphene Extreme Power Cord

Available in three configurations: Low Current Optimized (RED) for source components, High Current Optimized (BLUE) for power amplifiers, and Power Conditioner Optimized (YELLOW) for power strips and AC line conditioners. Each Graphene Extreme power cord contains:

  • Specific configurations for the Live [+] and Neutral [-] conductors optimized to match the actual functions of each leg.
  • Neutral backbone conductor employs Graphene, Silver, and OFC Copper.
  • Live backbone conductor employs Graphene, Silver, OFC Copper, Carbon Fiber, and Liquid Ceramic.
  • Red, Blue, and Yellow Optimization is achieved through differences in the reactive damping jackets. Individual conductors current limit at 30 amps.
