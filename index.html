Who We Are

Family owned and run since it's inception, Cerious Technologies is the brainchild of Robert Grost, a structural engineer and synthetic materials pioneer. After devising the world's first set of speakers designed with fluid filled stainless steel tubes and a cast ballistic ceramic layer, with a final skin of carbon fiber, the Cerious One was born. Cerious Technologies started the day these speakers were hooked up.

Cerious is a leader in the audio equipment community, focused on never-before-heard quality at an affordable price. Our cables are the best you'll find, and each one is handmade by our president and founder himself.

What We Offer

Digital Interconnects

Digital cables do not transfer analog signals, but rather digital signals. High frequency information must exist with limited samples per second due to the format, so if minor abnormalities exist in the low end, then there are enough samples to “fill in” proper information with little loss in integrity. Not so in high frequencies.

Digital equipment today is very sophisticated. It can communicate with other pieces in the chain, coordinating the utterly transparent cable between the sending unit and the receiving unit, allowing them to function as harmoniously as possible.

Interconnects

Graphene Extreme interconnects are small. The design goal was to maximize the individual conductors and make them as compact minimize noise and internal resonances.

Each conductor employs our Large Crystal Silver and Copper, 24,000 strands of carbon fiber and is filled with Graphene particles under high pressure. Graphene Extreme Interconnects are available either single ended terminate with Cardas RCAs or in Balanced configuration terminated with industry standard Neutrik XLRs.

Speaker Cables

No other cable in the Graphene Extreme line is as extreme as the speaker cable. Final tally – each conductor is ½” in diameter and effectively 2/0 AWG. Comprised of a core of 8 gauge Large Crystal Silver and Copper, wrapped in 96,000 strands of Carbon Fiber and flooded with Cerious Technologies proprietary Liquid Ceramic/Graphene Particle fluid, this bundle is then encased by copper shot which acts as both a conductor and a damping element within the jacket.

Each of the four individual tubes is reinforced with a wrap of Carbon Fiber to further damp vibrations and to ensure each Graphene Extreme cable performs like new for many years to come.

AC Power Cables

The Graphene Extreme power cables are an expression of everything we have learned in cable design. These cables are available in three different versions; Red, optimized for low current use (Preamps, digital front ends…), Blue for high current applications such as power amps, and Yellow, optimized for power conditioners.
